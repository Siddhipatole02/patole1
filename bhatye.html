Bhatye Beach

  • A tiny village situated close to Ratnagiri city, Bhatye is known for its 1.5 km long stretch of a straight and clear beach with flat sea showers. Bhatye Beach is one of the most secluded and serene beaches in Ratnagiri. The beach is not that crowded either. You can go for a morning walk, laze around and soak in the beauty, play beach ball or football or just stroll around collecting sea shells.
